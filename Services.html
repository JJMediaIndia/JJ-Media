OUR Services

Comprehensive production and post-production solutions tailored for cinema, television, and digital media.

Digital Intermediate (DI)

Our color grading suite is equipped to handle 4K and 8K workflows. We believe color is an emotion, not just an aesthetic. Our senior colorists work closely with Directors of Photography to establish the perfect visual tone for your narrative.

  • Theatrical Color Grading
  • HDR & Dolby Vision Mastering
  • Dailies & On-set Color Management

Creative Editing

The edit is the final rewrite. Our offline and online editing suites are designed for collaboration and speed. Whether it's a fast-paced commercial or a feature-length documentary, we find the rhythm in your footage.

  • Offline & Online Editing
  • Multi-cam workflows
  • Conforming & Mastering

Sound Design & Mixing

From the subtle rustle of leaves to earth-shattering explosions, we craft immersive soundscapes. Our studios are Dolby Atmos certified, ensuring your project sounds spectacular in any theater.

  • 5.1, 7.1 & Atmos Mixing
  • Foley Recording
  • Sound FX Editing

Dubbing & Voiceover

We offer multilingual dubbing services with a vast network of voice artists. Our acoustically treated recording booths ensure crystal-clear dialogue capture, preserving the original emotion of the performance.

  • Multilingual Dubbing
  • ADR (Automated Dialogue Replacement)
  • Voiceover Recording

Film Restoration

Preserving cinema history is our passion. We use advanced digital tools to remove dust, scratches, and flicker from old film stock, restoring it to its former glory—or even better—without losing its vintage character.

  • Dust & Scratch Removal
  • Flicker Reduction
  • Color Re-mastering

VFX & CGI

Our VFX supervisors and artists work from concept to final pixel. Whether it's invisible effects, set extensions, or full 3D character animation, we seamlessly blend the digital and the real.

  • Compositing & Rotoscopy
  • 3D Modeling & Animation
  • Motion Graphics
